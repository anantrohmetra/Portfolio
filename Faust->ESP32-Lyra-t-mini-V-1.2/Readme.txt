Audio-samples

Audio-samples/Audio:
Contains recordings of audio played through the minijack output of the LyraT Mini V1.2 board running Faust.
This includes a 440 Hz sawtooth wave and a melody generated using a sawtooth wave.

Audio-samples/Specogram-and-waveform:
Displays the sawtooth wave generated by Faust, shown both as a spectrogram and as a waveform.

Offloaded-folders-examples

Offloaded-folders-examples/Saw-wave-440hz:
This folder was successfully offloaded onto the LyraT Mini V1.2 board and produced a 440 Hz saw wave.

Offloaded-folders-examples/Saw-wave-melody:
This folder was successfully offloaded onto the LyraT Mini V1.2 board and generated a melody.
The sound source is a saw wave with mild saturation and delay.
