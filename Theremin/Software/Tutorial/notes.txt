DISTANCE & PITCH CONFIGURATION

These configurations are based on Leo Theremin's original design

the right hand controls the pitch (the musical note), and the left hand controls the volume

Frequency Range

The minimum value of the pitch is 0 hz when the distance between the sensor and the hand is 0mm. 

NOTE: This might be difficult to achieve practically because of physical contraints.

The maximum pitch when the distance is 350 mm is 2000 hz.

The default freq when there is no hand it should be 440hz

Amplitude Range

At a distance of 0 mm the amplitude will be bare minimum and close to silent.

At a distnace of 350 mm the amplitude will be maximum.

The default amplitude when there is no hand it should be maximum
